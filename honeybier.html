Honey Beir

School project 2018

About project

This project was for the Yoobee design school assignment, the concept was the Packaging design for my favorite fantasy world. I have chosen the book called "The city of dreaming books". This book is for the monster world and based on 17th-18th even before, my target audience and personas were not human for this project. The "Honey Beir" is combined English(Honey Beer) and German(Honig Beir) because this book was written by German originally. I want my packaging looks a hand-made style, I worked on illustration and calligraphy.
